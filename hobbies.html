Breakdancing and Volleyball

As the heading says, my hobbies are breakdancing and playing volleyball. I've been breaking for about 3 years now and am the vice president of the University of Virginia's breakdancing club, the Hooligans. While I am certainly not the best in the club, learning and having fun with friends is why I joined!
I started playing volleyball in my freshman year of college, and I play casually with friends and compete in Intra-Mural games at school. Unfortunately, I didn't play much during my second year of college due to the Covid-19 pandemic, but I hope to play much more in the future with friends.
